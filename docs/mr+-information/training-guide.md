# Training Guide

:::info

The objective of this guide is to provide Executive Assistant+ with instructions on training sessions.

:::

### <font color="#C21807">Training Behavior and Information</font>

* Hosts should avoid rushing through the script. Provide sufficient time for LRs to read and process the information being presented.
* Hosts may remove any LRs who speak after :00 from the server.

### <font color="#C21807">Rank Information</font>

Below is an overview of each rank's job at training sessions.

| Job        | Description          | Eligible Ranks | Quantity |
|:-------------|:------------------|:------|:-------------------|
| Host | The Host should join the Training Center between :35 and :40 and claim the session using the GUI at :45. The Host leads the session by delivering the introduction, cafe information, and register information. The Host is also in charge of ranking all LRs. | Executive Assistant+ | **1** host per training |
| Announcer | The announcer is tasked with making the announcement about the training session in all store servers that have at least 40 players. The selection of the announcer is made by the Host, who has the authority to choose an individual for this specific role. | Store Director+ | **1** announcer per training
| Lower Ranks | Low Ranks (LRs), which are Trainee Team Members, Junior Team Members, and Senior Team Members, attend training sessions. By doing so, LRs have the opportunity to receive a possible promotion to Department Supervisor. | Trainee Team Member, Junior Team Member, and Senior Team Member | No maximum |
| Spectators | Spectators supervise training sessions and ensure everything is running smoothly. | Group Administrator+ | No maximum |

## <font color="#C21807">Host Script</font>

This section provides an overview of the responsibilities and duties assigned to the Host, as well as guidelines on what they should say.

### <font color="#C21807">Host: Before Claiming</font>

Prior to the commencement of the training session, it is the responsibility of the Host to find an HR who will be responsible for announcing the training session in all store servers that have at least 40 players. The Host should ensure that the HR uses the appropriate format when making the announcement.

Between :35 and :40, it is expected that the Host arrives at the Training Center to prepare for the training session. It is the responsibility of the Host to ensure they are online and punctual for the session. Failing to attend a claimed session without giving a notice will result in a punishment (if done repetitively).

### <font color="#C21807">Host: Claiming</font>

At :45, which is 15 minutes before the training session begins, the Host should claim the session. Simultaneously, the announcer is responsible for making an announcement about the training session in all store servers that have at least 40 players.


### <font color="#C21807">Host: Training Commences</font>

At :00, which is the exact time of the training session's commencement, the Host should type the command "/e :slock".

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Greetings, everyone, and welcome to today's training session!*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Now that training has begun, all chatting must stop. Any messages sent from this point forward will result in a removal from the server.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *During this session, you will be given important information about the procedures for working in BloxStreet's cafe and checkout areas.

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *After completing this training, you will receive the Department Supervisor rank.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *We will begin with the cafe procedures.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Due to Roblox chat restrictions, you may be unable to communicate with certain customers. If a customer cannot chat with you, please direct them to the self-ordering machines.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *To log into the POS system, scan your Staff Card when prompted.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *When a customer approaches your cafe register, it is essential to greet them politely and professionally.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Here is an example of a greeting: "Hello! Welcome to BloxStreet Corporation! I'm (username), and I'll be assisting you today. How may I help you?"*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Be sure to include the term "BloxStreet" in your greeting.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *You may use the !notepad command to write down customer orders.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *When the customer finishes ordering, scan the items they requested.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Afterwards, click "Finalize Transaction" and prompt the customer to scan their Cash Card.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *After payment, wish the customer an enjoyable rest of their day.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Always sign out of your register before leaving the cafe.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *To begin making an order, claim it on the easyPOS screen. If there is no staff username attached, click "Claim Order" and an enlarged version of the customer’s order will appear.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *To prepare sandwiches, click the cutting board to begin. If an order exceeds seven toppings, select the most important ones to equal seven.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *To prepare drinks, use a grande cup. Follow the drink boards and include any additional ingredients requested.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *To hand the customer their order, use the /handto (username) or /give (username) commands, the hand button on the left side of your screen, or press the H key on your keyboard.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *After completing the order, click the "Complete" button on the easyPOS screen.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *We will now move on to the checkout procedures.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *When a customer approaches your checkout register, greet them politely and professionally.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Before assisting with a purchase, equip your Staff Card from your inventory and swipe it on the computer. This only needs to be done once.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *After confirming you are signed in, have the customer scan their Loyalty Card. The computer will automatically detect their items and display them on your screen.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Click "Finalize Transaction" in the top right corner of the screen and select "Pay with Cash Card."*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Once payment is complete, the customer will receive their items. At this point, wish them an enjoyable rest of their day.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *To sign out, click "Sign Out" before moving to another station.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *At this point, all essential information for your success has been covered.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *If you encounter any trollers in the store, report them to an available Store Director or higher.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *For further assistance, refer to the group’s social links or speak with an in-game HR.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *To receive future promotions, you must attend shifts and demonstrate professionalism, strong grammar, and hard work.

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *Shifts are hosted daily, and the full schedule is available in the staff room.*

<font color="#0F52BA">**Host:**</font> *I will now approach each of you individually to rank you. Do not leave the server.*

The Host will now rank the LRs to Department Supervisor. If there are more than 20 LRs, the Host may request assistance from 1-3 Executive Assistants or Store Executives for ranking purposes. 

The Host should run the :info command on each LR before kicking. Running the command ensures each LR has been ranked correctly.

### <font color="#C21807">Training Conclusion</font>

The Host should leave the server once all LRs have been ranked.
